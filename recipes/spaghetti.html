Baked Spaghetti

Description

Baked spaghetti is a comforting, hearty and crowd-pleasing dish that combines the flavos of classic spaghetti dinner with the convenience and appeal of a casserole.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Beef and Onion
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef and onion together, then drain off the excess oil.
  3. Add the sauce, salt and pepper. Whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and butter in a separate bowl.
  4. Toss the noodles in the mixture.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a prepared baking dish.
  6. Cover and bake for 40 minutes. Sprinkle with mozzarella, then keep baking until the cheese is melted.
