Crispy Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

This recipe features a tender, juicy chicken breast perfectly seasoned and fried to golden-brown perfection. Nestled in a soft toasted bun, this sandwich is topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes and crunchy pickles, all complemeted by a creamy mayo.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves
  • Buttermilk
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder
  • All purpose flour
  • Cooking Oil
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Mayonaise
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken breast horizontally into 2 fillets. Place the fillets in a resealable zip-top bag with buttermilk, buffalo sauce and garlic powder. Seal and shake to blend the ingredients evenly. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Combine flour, poultry seasoning, and pepper in a shallow dish or pie plate. Blend with a fork or whisk.
  4. Remove chicken from the marinade and shake off excess. Dip chicken in the breading mixture until evenly coated on both sides. Discard the remaining marinade.
  5. Add cooking oil to a large skillet so that it thickly coats the bottom and heat over medium-high heat until sizzling but not smoking. Place 2 chicken fillets in the skillet so they fit comfortably; do not overcrowd. Fry until crispy and golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 8 to 10 minutes.
  7. Remove chicken from oven. Place a chicken fillet on the bottom half of a bun. Add Buffalo sauce to taste. Top with pickle slices, tomato, and lettuce. Spread mayonaise on the inside of the top bun and place over the sandwich. Repeat to make remaining sandwiches.
